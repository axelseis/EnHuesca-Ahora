ArtNearMe Brisbane is a location-based mobile app to help you discover public art installations in and around Brisbane city centre. The app is designed for mobile phones, but also provides a rich experience on desktop computers. Brisbane has an extensive public art collection just waiting to be discovered.

The app was created by Colin Gourlay. Your feedback helps improve the app for everyone. Please send your comments and suggestions to colin.j.gourlay@gmail.com, or send a tweet to:

@artnearmeapp or @collypops

About hack::Brisbane

ArtNearMe Brisbane was originally built as an entry for hack::Brisbane, a competition held by Brisbane City Council which challenged the development communuity to improve Brisbane by building apps/websites/tools around public datasets.

The public art data is provided by the Brisbane Datastore Public Art dataset, which is licensed under the Creative Commons Attribution 3.0.
